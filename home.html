About me:

Hi! I'm Lincoln. I'm studying electrical and computer engineering and am working towards a double degree in physics at the University of Maryland. I work at Base 2 Engineering as a computer engineering intern, where I write low-level software in C. I love working on low-level software because its satisfying to see everything come together, which is another reason I love physics and maths so much (which are two of my favorite pasttimes outside the computer engineering field). I also love Arduino and electrical engineering, so I started an Arduino club in high school.
